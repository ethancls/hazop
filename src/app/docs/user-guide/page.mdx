
# User Guide

## Creating a Project
1. Navigate to your Organization Dashboard.
2. Click **New Project**.
3. Enter the project name and description.
4. Click **Create**.

## Using AI Assistance
HAZOP Labs integrates with AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Ollama) to accelerate your study.

### Generating Nodes
1. Go to the **Nodes** tab of your project.
2. Ensure you have entered a detailed **Project Description** or **Process Description**.
3. Click **Generate with AI**.
4. The system will analyze your description and propose:
   - Logical study nodes
   - Design intents
   - Recommended parameters
   - Connections between nodes
5. Review and accept the generated nodes.

### Analyzing Deviations
1. Open a specific Node.
2. Click **Add Deviation** or ask the AI to **Analyze Node**.
3. The AI will suggest potential deviations, causes, and consequences based on the node's design intent and parameters.

## Action Tracking
1. For any high-risk deviation, add a **Recommendation**.
2. Assign it to a team member (if applicable).
3. Track the status (Open, In Progress, Closed) in the **Summary** tab.
